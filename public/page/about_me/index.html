Just more bits about me

Hi there!

My name is Christian, I am a Senior Software Engineer based in Berlin where I am organizing PyCon DE 2019 and berlin.python.pizza.

My focus is currently on:

  • Microservices and event-driven architecture
  • Machine/Statistical learning
  • DevOps for microservices and machine learning
  • Data engineering

I am a proud Pythonista and Python Software Foundation Fellow.

During the 2015-2018 period I had the opportunity the help the Python Community in Europe as a board member of the EuroPython Society, core organizer of 4 editions of the EuroPython Conference and conference chair of EuroPython 2017.
